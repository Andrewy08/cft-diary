Soothing pause

Step 2 of 5 — Ground yourself before responding

Soothing rhythm breathing
Slow your breathing — in for 4 counts, hold, out for 6. Let your body settle. Bring your compassionate image to mind. Tap the circle to begin.
Tap to begin
4 cycles · ~50 seconds
Function of the critic
What is the self-critical voice trying to do?
Notice it with curiosity — is it trying to motivate you, protect you, warn you? What function does it serve?

Compassionate response

Step 3 of 5 — Respond with warmth and wisdom

What does your compassionate image have to say about this?
What would you say to a close friend who was thinking and feeling this way?
What does the compassionate part of you want to say to the self-critical part?

Broader perspective

Step 4 of 5 — Widen the lens

What are some other, kinder or more realistic ways of viewing this situation?
How will you feel about this in a week? A month? A year?
What do you actually need right now?

Compassionate conclusion

Step 5 of 5 — Bring it together

Read back through your compassionate responses. Now write a kinder, wiser conclusion.
Say it to yourself in a warm, caring tone — as you would to someone you love.
